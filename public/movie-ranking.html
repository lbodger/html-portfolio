Top Disney Films According to Me!

My favourite Disney films

Aladdin

The original 1993 version.
This has the absolute best songs, and the wonderful Robin Williams as the Genie. And Rajah... well, he is just gorgeous.

The Jungle Book

Again, the original version.
The songs, the animation, the characters. It's just a really lovely take on the Kipling original.

Moana

I am Moana!!!

Incredible story of a girl refusing to do what is expected of her without looking at other options. Yet she still has the best interests of the people around her at heart.
Also Maui is probably the hottest animated demi-god ever.

Inside Out

Yeah yeah, I know it's Pixar. But it still counts. Well, I hope it does. I just love the personification of the emotions. I love how they look, how they behave.
